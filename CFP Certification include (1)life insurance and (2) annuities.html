Yes! The CFP Certification curriculum definitely includes both life insurance and annuities as key components of the Risk Management and Insurance Planning topic.

1. Life Insurance in CFP

  • Understanding different types of life insurance: term life, whole life, universal life, variable life, etc.
  • How life insurance fits into a client’s overall financial plan — protection needs, risk transfer, and estate planning.
  • Analyzing insurance policy features, costs, benefits, and underwriting considerations.
  • Tax implications of life insurance policies.

2. Annuities in CFP

  • Various annuity products: fixed, variable, indexed, immediate, deferred annuities.
  • How annuities provide guaranteed income streams, especially for retirement planning.
  • Understanding fees, surrender charges, payout options, and tax treatment.
  • Suitability and role of annuities in client portfolios.

These topics are tested as part of the Risk Management and Insurance Planning section in the CFP exam and are crucial for comprehensive financial planning advice.
