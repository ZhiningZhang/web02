What is Kaplan CFP Review?

Kaplan Financial Education offers a comprehensive, structured Certified Financial Planner (CFP) exam prep program designed to help candidates pass the CFP Certification Exam. It’s used by thousands of students globally and is known for its quality content, flexible learning options, and strong student support.

Key Features

1. Comprehensive Study Materials

  • Textbooks & Study Notes covering all CFP exam topics (Professional Conduct, General Principles, Investment Planning, Tax Planning, Retirement, Estate, Insurance, and more).
  • Clear explanations aligned with the CFP Board’s Principal Knowledge Topics.
  • Includes examples, charts, and practice questions.

2. Video Lectures

  • Engaging video lessons led by expert instructors.
  • Break down complex topics into understandable segments.
  • Available on-demand so you can study anytime.

3. Practice Questions & Exams

  • Thousands of multiple-choice questions to reinforce learning.
  • Full-length mock exams simulate the real exam experience.
  • Detailed answer explanations to understand mistakes.

4. Study Tools & Mobile Access

  • Mobile-friendly platform lets you study on the go.
  • Progress tracking and customizable study plans.
  • Flashcards, quizzes, and summaries to reinforce key concepts.

5. Live & On-Demand Options

  • You can choose between:

    • Self-Paced (fully on-demand study at your own pace)
    • Live Online Classes (scheduled sessions with instructors for Q&A)
    • In-Person Classes (available in select locations)

6. Support & Coaching

  • Access to expert instructors for questions.
  • Study coaching and guidance to keep you on track.
  • Online forums and student communities.

Pricing

  • Prices vary based on package and format.
  • Typical cost range: $2,000 – $3,000 for full prep packages (textbooks, videos, exams).
  • Sometimes discounts or payment plans are available.

Who is it For?

  • Ideal for candidates who want a structured and complete CFP exam prep solution.
  • Suitable for beginners and those with some financial background.
  • Flexible learning modes fit different schedules and learning styles.

Benefits

  • Practice exams help build confidence and exam endurance.
  • Support resources help reduce overwhelm and confusion.

What Others Say

  • Live classes and instructor access are praised for clarifying tough topics.
